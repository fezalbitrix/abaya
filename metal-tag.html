Metal Tag

Metal tags are durable and stylish branding solutions, designed to showcase your brand name or logo with a premium finish. Made from high-quality metals like steel or brass, these tags are built to last and resist wear. Available in gold, silver, and custom colors, they add a luxurious touch to abayas, clothing, and accessories. The logo can be sewn or glued onto fabric, ensuring a secure and long-lasting attachment. Perfect for brands looking to create a high-end, professional look, metal tags make your products stand out with elegance and quality.
