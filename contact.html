FAQ

Minimum order quantities (MOQs) vary based on the product. While many items start with a minimum order requirement of 1,000 pieces, the MOQ can differ across our range.

A full list of minimum order quantities for each product can be found here.

Experiencing our products firsthand can help you make informed decisions. To request a sample pack or to discuss which product areas you'd like to explore, please get in touch and we can provide details regarding the cost associated with your preferred selection of products.

Our team provides quotes tailored to your specific designs. There may be initial development costs involved which cover aspects such as creation of moulds or screens, setting up of machines and purchase of materials. Within these development costs, samples are provided.

Every item we produce is tailored to meet your individual specifications and requirements. As a result, we do not offer a set price list since each product is unique.

To receive pricing information, we require your designs and detailed specifications. Our team will then create a personalized quotation based on your specific requirements.

Please note that at this time, we do not offer a design service. For guidance on design specifications or to access helpful resources, we encourage you to visit our resources page.

This page contains valuable information and resources to assist you in preparing detailed design specifications for your products.

Please provide editable vector files in AI (Adobe Illustrator), PDF, or EPS formats. Additionally, ensure that all text elements are converted to outlines.

For any photographic elements included in the design, it's essential that they have a minimum resolution of 300dpi (dots per inch). This ensures high-quality reproduction and clarity in the final product.

Our production facilities are situated in the Dongguan area of southern China. This strategic location enables us to leverage a network of factory partners, enabling us to offer specialised production services across our entire product range.

Our delivery charges are determined based on your location. To ensure competitive pricing and efficient delivery we work with several trusted courier services.

We strive to offer flexible options for shipping. If you have a preferred courier service, you can nominate them for the delivery of your order.

We're dedicated to providing options that align with your commitment to sustainability and therefore offer sustainable alternatives across many of our produce areas, ensuring you can make environmentally conscious choices.

To explore our range of sustainable products and alternatives, please visit our sustainability page.
